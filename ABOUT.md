# About Skool Community CLI

The **Skool Community CLI** is a specialized tool designed for community owners, educators, and power users who need more than what the standard web interface provides. 

## 🎯 The Mission
Our mission is to empower Skool creators with **data sovereignty** and **operational efficiency**. By moving community management to the command line, we enable a level of automation and analysis that was previously impossible.

## 🏗️ Technical Architecture
Built with performance and security in mind:
- **Go Core**: High-concurrency engine for rapid data synchronization.
- **SQLite Persistence**: Your data stays on your machine, enabling fast local queries without rate-limiting issues.
- **Modern Networking**: Implements HTTP/2 and production-grade headers to ensure seamless connectivity with Skool's infrastructure.

## 🌟 Why Use This?
- **Speed**: Manage content and members without waiting for browser UI to load.
- **Insights**: Go beyond basic metrics with predictive churn analysis and contributor velocity tracking.
- **Automation**: Easily script repetitive tasks or integrate Skool data into your other workflows.

## 🤝 Community & Support
This project is an open-source initiative dedicated to the Skool ecosystem. We believe that better tools lead to better communities.
